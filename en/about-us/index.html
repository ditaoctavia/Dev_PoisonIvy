CumulusPro

Transforming the lives of documents in the cloud.

What does it mean to transform the lives of documents in the cloud?

To a home user,

it means sharing – with colleagues, peers, family and friends.

To a small business user,

it means ease – of configuration and implementation.

To a corporate user,

it means efficiency – for processes, paperwork and people.

As a technology company, CumulusPro is focused on creating accessible products to meet real needs. Our team of professionals have decades of experience and expertise in areas such as cloud computing, document processing and workflow, Software as a Service (SaaS), business intelligence and strategic development, solution selling, and more – which is demonstrated through our suite of web-based apps for both individuals and businesses.

With the free web-based software Scan+Share, we look to simplify the way documents are captured and shared on the cloud. So, home and small business users can scan their documents and share them directly on Google Drive, Evernote, and OneDrive.

With the seamless integration of Scan+Process Lite to existing Line of Business application, we empower businesses to scan their documents at source for optimum security and efficiency. This web-based scanning app is quick and easy to configure, with flexible pricing options and no training cost required. With the dynamic Scan+Process, we transform the grind of paperwork into cost-efficient, automated systems on the cloud.

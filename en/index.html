CumulusPro Scan+Share

For all home users and home businesses

CumulusPro Scan+Share provides home users such as you with a workspace to scan, email and print your documents, save them to your PC or external drive or share them on the cloud – all for free!

CumulusPro Scan+Share is a fast, friendly and free web-based scanning app that offers you the ease and convenience of scanning with any TWAIN-compliant device, and the ability to save your documents in JPG, PDF, TIFF or ZIP format. What's more, you can share your scanned documents instantly on Google Drive, Evernote, and OneDrive. Soon, you'll also be able to share them on Dropbox and more!

If you're currently using CumulusPro Scan+Store, your app will be automatically upgraded to CumulusPro Scan+Share when you next launch it in your browser.

How can Scan+Share help me?

It's free

Get started right away, and never have to pay for a subscription or an upgrade – or anything at all!

Completely web-based

No technical knowledge required! Configuration is automatic, and the app is up-to-date everytime you launch it.

Run on a choice of browsers

Google Chrome, Firefox, Internet Explorer 9, or Internet Explorer 10 – simply choose the one that works best for you.

Use your current operating system

Works with any TWAIN-compliant scanner or MFP, and PC that supports Windows XP, Vista, Windows 7 or Windows 8.

Scan as much as you wish

Utility bills? Hotel receipts? Claims? Educational certificates? Just scan your documents and organise them easily.

Share on the cloud

Connect to your account to share instantly on Google Drive, Evernote, and OneDrive - and soon, to Dropbox and more!

CumulusPro Scan+Process Lite

For organisations and service providers who scan documents in single or multiple locations, and require optimum security through the line

If you're a business owner or user, CumulusPro Scan+Process Lite empowers you to scan, index the data fields on your documents, and upload them to your existing Line of Business (LOB) application.

CumulusPro Scan+Process Lite is designed for easy integration and enables a direct, secure connection with your existing Line of Business (LOB) application, portal, or website system. What's more, you can upload your scanned documents directly from the app to SharePoint, and to other business applications.

How can Scan+Process Lite help my business?

Simple configuration and operation

It's quick and easy to start scanning in critical applications for business, with no manual configuration required. The app is ready-to-use in just minutes!

Greater security

Scan your transactional and ad-hoc documents, and import them at source. Your documents never have to leave the security of your existing business system.

Dynamic set-up of index field name

The app is configured to fetch the same index field name already set up in your existing LOB application, so there's no need for reconfiguration within the app.

Seamless integration to existing systems

The app is designed to integrate quickly, smoothly, and securely with your current LOB application or backend system, whatever they may be.

Latest version always available

The app is always up-to-date, every time it's launched on a browser. Never pay for a new version, or lose out by using an old one.

Flexible pricing options

No upfront expenditure on software licenses or building an app from scratch, so your investment is made over time and converted from Capex to Opex.

Privacy Policy

At CumulusPro, your privacy is of the utmost importance to us. Please take the time to read and understand our Privacy Policy, and if you have any questions, please get in touch with us.

Our Privacy Policy describes:

  • The information we collect from you.
  • How we use this information.
  • What we do with information collected on social media pages.
  • Your options on accessing and updating the information you share with us.

Information we collect from you

Generally, you can use our apps without revealing any personally identifiable information. However, from time to time, we collect information from users such as you to provide better, and more relevant services and features on our apps. This information is collected in two ways:

  • What you give us. For example, when you voluntarily share your email address and/or 'Like' our social media pages such as Facebook and LinkedIn.
  • What we gather from your use of our apps. We may collect information about the apps you use and how you use them – such as the way you respond to new features on our apps, or the ads on our webpages. This includes:
    • Device information. We may collect device-specific information (such as your hardware model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers).
    • Log information. This may include:
      • Details of how you use our app, such as your scanning activities and document generating selections.
      • Internet Protocol (IP) address, which is a numerical label assigned to your device (such as a computer or multi-function printer), participating in a computer network that uses the Internet Protocol for communication.
      • Device event information such as crashes, system activity, hardware settings, browser type, browser language, the date and time of your request and referral URL.
      • Cookies, which are pieces of data stored on your hard drive containing information about you, which may uniquely identify the type of browser you use (such as Internet Explorer 9, Google Chrome or Firefox). Generally, cookies do not permit us to personally identify you.
    • Location information. We may request that you share your geographical location upon registration. We will not determine your location through other means, such as using sensor data from your device to identify nearby Wi-Fi access points and cell towers.
    • Unique application numbers. Certain apps may include a unique application number. This number and information about your installation (for example, the operating system type and application version number) may be sent to CumulusPro when you install or uninstall that app or when that app periodically contacts our servers.
    • Local storage. We may collect information locally on your device using mechanisms such as browser web storage and app data caches. Such information will not be transmitted to and/or stored on our servers.
    • Cookies and anonymous identifiers. We collect and store information in various ways, which may include sending one or more cookies or anonymous identifiers to your device. This may also be done when you interact with the services we offer through our partners.

How we use the information we collect from you

We collect information so as to maintain and improve our apps, develop new ones, and protect ourselves (CumulusPro) and all our users.

As such, when you get in touch with us at CumulusPro, we may keep a record of your communication in order to resolve the issues you may be encountering. We may use also use your email address to inform you about changes and/or enhancements to existing apps and the launch of new apps.

To improve the quality of our app, and your overall user experience, we make use of the information we collect from cookies and other technologies such as pixel tags.

Your personal information may be processed on a server located outside the country where you live.

If we need to use information we collect from you for any purpose other than those set out in this Privacy Policy, we will seek your prior consent.

Information collected on social media pages

When you 'Like' any of our social media pages, such as Facebook and/or LinkedIn, and interact with the features and applications on those pages, you allow us to access certain information from your profile on that social media site.

This information may vary based on your interaction, the social media site platform, and your privacy settings on that site. You can find out more about these from the social media site where you interact with us.

Information we may collect from your interaction with our page on a social media page includes:

  • Your first and last name
  • Your profile picture and/or its URL
  • Your user ID name and/or number, which is linked to publicly available information such as your name and/or profile photo
  • The user ID names and/or numbers of your friends who are connected to our social media page
  • The login email address you provide to the social media site when you register
  • Your physical location and that of your access devices
  • Your gender
  • Your birthday

By interacting with our social media page or pages, you authorise us to collect, store and use in accordance with this Privacy Policy any and all information that you agree the social media site can provide to us through the social media site Application Programming Interface (API). Your agreement begins when you "Accept" or "Allow" (or similar terms) one of our applications and/or pages on a social media site.

To manage the information we receive about you from the social media site, follow the instructions on that social media site for updating your information and changing your privacy settings.

Once we receive your information from a social media site, that information is stored and used accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Transparency and choice

We realise that your privacy concerns may be different from another user's. This is why we strive to be clear about the information we collect and how we use it, so that you can make meaningful choices about what you would like to provide.

You may also set your browser to block all cookies, including cookies associated with our services, or to indicate when a cookie is being set by us. However, it's important to remember that many of our services may not function properly if your cookies are disabled.

Accessing and updating your personal information

Whenever you use our app, we aim to provide you with access to your personal information. If that information is incorrect, we strive to enable you to update it quickly or delete it – unless we have to store that information for legitimate business or legal purposes. When updating your personal information, we may ask you to verify your identity before we can act on your request.

When you request for your personal information to be updated or changed, we have the right to reject the request if deemed unreasonably repetitive, require disproportionate technical effort (for example, developing a new system or fundamentally changing an existing practice), risk the privacy of others, or would be extremely impractical.

Where we can provide easy access to and correction of information, we will do so for free. If it requires a disproportionate effort, we have the right to charge for the service. We aim to maintain our services in a manner that protects information from accidental or malicious destruction. Because of this, after you delete information from our services, we may not immediately delete residual copies from our active servers and may not remove information from our backup systems.

Information we share

We do not share personal information with companies, organisations and individuals outside of our company (CumulusPro) unless:

  • External information processing is required.
    Personal information may be provided to our affiliates or other trusted businesses or persons for processing purposes, based on our specific instructions and in compliance with our Privacy Policy and any other appropriate confidentiality and security measures.
  • We are bound by legal obligations.
    We will share personal information with companies, organisations or individuals outside of our company (CumulusPro) if we have a good-faith belief that access, use, preservation or disclosure of the information is reasonably necessary to:
    • meet any applicable law, regulation, legal process or enforceable governmental request.
    • enforce applicable Terms of Use, including investigation of potential violations.
    • detect, prevent, or otherwise address fraud, security or technical issues.
    • protect against harm to the rights, property or safety of CumulusPro, our users or the public as required or permitted by law.

We may share aggregated, non-personally identifiable information publicly and with our partners – like publishers, advertisers or connected sites. For example, we may share information publicly to show trends about the general use of our app.

If we (CumulusPro) are involved in a merger, acquisition or asset sale, we will continue to ensure the confidentiality of any personal information and give affected users notice before personal information is transferred or becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

Information security

We take stringent steps to protect ourselves (CumulusPro) and all our users from unauthorised access to or unauthorised alteration, disclosure or destruction of information we hold, including:

  • Many of our apps and services are encrypted using SSL.
  • Developing our apps to work with Internet browsers that include the Safe Browsing feature – Internet Explorer 9, Google Chrome and Firefox.
  • Constantly reviewing our information collection, storage and processing practices, including physical security measures, to guard against unauthorised access to systems.
  • Restricting access to personal information to our employees, contractors and agents who need to know that information in order to process it for us, and who are subject to strict contractual confidentiality obligations and may be disciplined or terminated if they fail to meet these obligations.

Application

Our Privacy Policy applies to all of the apps we offer as well as those offered by our affiliates, including services offered on other sites, but excludes services that have separate privacy policies that do not incorporate this Privacy Policy.

Our Privacy Policy does not apply to services offered by other companies or individuals, including those linked from our apps and web pages. Our Privacy Policy does not cover the information practices of other companies and organisations who advertise our services, and who may use cookies, pixel tags and other technologies to serve and offer ads.

Enforcement

We make it a point to regularly review our compliance with our Privacy Policy. We also adhere to several self-regulatory frameworks, and work closely with the appropriate regulatory authorities, including local data protection authorities, to resolve any complaints regarding the transfer of personal data that we cannot resolve with our users directly.

Changes

Our Privacy Policy may change from time to time. Your rights under this Privacy Policy will never be reduced without your explicit and written consent. Any Privacy Policy changes will be posted on this page. If the changes are significant, we will deliver a more direct notice – including, but not limited to, email notification where we have your email address on our record. Prior versions of this Privacy Policy will always be available in an archive for your review.

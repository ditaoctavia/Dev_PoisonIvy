Scan+Process

CumulusPro Scan+Process is a completely cloud-based Document Processing Automation (DPA) platform that enables a partner community to provide cost-efficient solutions to their customers. It is built to intuitively design processes that are uniquely relevant to each business, with both transactional and batch processing capabilities that enable users to access documents remotely for approval, retrieval, and more.

The architecture of CumulusPro Scan+Process is what sets it apart from other platforms on the market right now. It is built upon three layers – Infrastructure, Technology, and Application.

  • Infrastructure
  • Technology
  • Application

Infrastructure is at the foundation of CumulusPro Scan+Process, which refers to a cloud platform that guarantees optimum stability and security at every step of the process – whether it is performed locally or remotely.

Built on the secure and stable Microsoft Azure cloud infrastructure. With the support of multiple high-security Microsoft Azure datacentres, Scan+Process assures users of round-the-clock security and reliability, in any time zone, anywhere the world. The data that passes through our servers uses AES-256 encryption, and all data communication via Scan+Process is also encrypted with HTTPS protection – assuring optimum security, from start to end.

Zero installation of server-side processes. All server-side processes, including document Classification, Extraction, and Automated Quality Control, are operated off our Microsoft Azure-supported cloud infrastructure, so there is no installation, maintenance, or support staff required to manage these processes.

Completely web-based, for fast deployment. Scan+Process ensures quick and easy deployment to any location in the world. It also uses less space on computers than a locally-installed client, without compromising on speed and stability.

The next layer is Technology, which refers to the industry-leading components that make up this platform and deliver its array of dynamic and intelligent features. These components are also designed to integrate seamlessly with existing systems.

Works as a fully-featured Business Process Management (BPM) platform. Process flows can be as simple or elaborate as they need to be. Processes such as multiple routing options as well as assignment of activities and documents to groups, roles and individual users can be configured directly by the process owners – with the use of a simple and intuitive flow designer, and without any programming or coding required.

Seamless integration with existing systems. Scan+Process integrates and engages in two-way communication with any third party system, throughout the entire document management process. For users who have some existing processes that need to be a part of the Document Processing Automation but have been installed and configured on-premise, these processes can continue to be executed as is while working with Scan+Process on the cloud.

Automatic document classification. An intelligent classification engine sorts documents according to type (such as an invoice) based on their contents. The documents can then be sent quickly, conveniently,and automatically to various processes, repositories, or individuals within the company.

The final layer is Application, which refers to the ease of the end-user experience. It focuses on reducing cost and risk for the user, promoting efficiency, and increasing overall satisfaction.

No compromise to performance. Whether it is Scanning, Rescanning, Validation, or Data Entry, interaction is fast and easy as users enjoy the same response time as when they are using locally-installed clients.

Graphical process designer. A web-based graphical process designer empowers users to configure business processes according to their requirements without any technical knowledge. This also ensures that business processes can be up and running more efficiently, without the long wait for programming and coding deployment.
